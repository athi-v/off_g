The Practical Guide to

Offshore Investing

This Guide provides insight on how to use current offshore structures; it addresses misgivings and confusion; and unpacks emerging and existing market trends that will reveal the true value of offshore investment.

BEYOND REMARKABLE

This Publication is accredited for
13 CPD Hours by the Financial Planning Institute.

The international planning landscape has changed to such an extent that the decision where to live and where to do business and invest, may have serious consequences for the family or individual involved.

Knowledge of the Offshore landscape, together with careful planning in advance are absolute essentials.

This up-to-date, purposeful and comprehensive publication is essential to anyone who is invested offshore or who has aspirations to do so. It is a valuable tool in any financial adviser's armoury!
